# FinVitals — Financial Health Intelligence for Government

**By [FinMango](https://finmango.org) · 501(c)(3)**

Governments can't protect what they can't see. FinVitals adds an AI intelligence layer on top of FinMango's Financial Health Barometer — turning real-time data into an early warning system that predicts community crises, identifies at-risk households, and connects them to benefits before they fall through the cracks.

## Live Tools

Already built and deployed:

- **[Financial Health Barometer](https://finmango.org/barometer.html)** — Real-time financial stress tracking across all 50 states
- **[Food Assistance Calculator](https://finmango.org/food-assistance-calculator)** — Cross-program eligibility screening (SNAP, WIC, pantries, utilities)
- **[Food Desert Analyzer](https://finmango.org/food-desert-analyzer)** — Grocery access and food availability mapping by zip code
- **[Affordability Lab](https://finmango.org/affordability-lab.html)** — Interactive housing affordability analysis

## Prototype Tools

Demonstrating the architecture's extensibility to disaster recovery:

- **[Disaster Aid Finder](disaster-aid-finder.html)** — 8-question intake → personalized list of disaster aid programs
- **[Recovery Roadmap](recovery-roadmap.html)** — Interactive filing sequence timeline with dependency chain and deadlines

## How FinVitals Works

1. **Predict** — AI analyzes Barometer signals to identify communities approaching crisis
2. **Identify** — Cross-reference population signals with eligibility data to find who's at risk
3. **Connect** — Proactively reach affected households with programs they qualify for
4. **Monitor** — Track policy changes and model their impact in real time
